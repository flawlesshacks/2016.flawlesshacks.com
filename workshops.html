"The Evolution of a Payments System"

by Jessica Yao, Payments Experience engineer, from

Presentation Room

3:00 - 4:00pm

As much as we would like to fancy it a work of intelligent design, software is oftentimes a process of evolution---messy, organic, and dependent on the ecosystem in which it is produced. There is a delicate equilibrium that is maintained from balancing a solid infrastructure with the rapid development of new features, and along the way, vestigial features will almost certainly be left behind. In this talk, I will describe these "tailbones" in the context of the Airbnb payments system, and how we've met the demands of user growth by shifting from a monolithic Rails application to a microservice architecture, from batch processing to stream processing, and so on and so forth.
