FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


Who can attend?

All undergraduate and graduate students who identify as women or gender non-conforming from any school are welcome. If you are under 18, we’ll need a parental consent form.


Will you be providing travel reimbursements?

Unfortunately, we will not be providing reimbursements for the hackathon.


I don’t have a team. Can I still participate?

Of course! It is absolutely not necessary to have a team prior to coming, and we can hook you up with a team when you get here.


How big can a team be?

Team sizes are flexible but we suggest a max of 4 people per team.


How do I get there?

FlawlessHacks will be held at RiseNY in New York City. The address is 43 W 23rd Street, nearby to the F, M, 1, N and R trains.
